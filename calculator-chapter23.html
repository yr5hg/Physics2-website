2. Electric Field Calculator

Formula: \( E = \frac{k_e |q|}{r^2} \)

3. Electric Field of a Uniformly Charged Rod

Formula: \( E = \frac{k_e Q}{a(\ell + a)} \)

4. Electric Field of a Uniform Ring of Charge

Formula: \( E = \frac{k_e x Q}{(a^2 + x^2)^{3/2}} \)

5. Electric Field of a Uniformly Charged Disk

Formula: \( E_x = 2 \pi k_e \sigma \left( 1 - \frac{x}{(R^2 + x^2)^{1/2}} \right) \)
